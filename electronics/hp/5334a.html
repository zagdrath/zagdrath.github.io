HP 5334A Universal Counter

The HP 5334A Universal Counter is a two-channel, 100 MHz universal counter offering 9-digit resolution with configurable gate time and 2 ns single shot time interval resolution. Standard measurements include, frequency, period, rise time, fall time, pulse width, and peak amplitude. Options include a high-stability time base, a digital voltmeter, a 1.3 GHz C-channel, and rear panel inputs.
