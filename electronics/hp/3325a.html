HP 3325A Synthesizer/Function Generator

The HP 3325A Synthesizer/Function Generator offers 11 digit resolution, a function generator with precise waveforms, a wideband sweeper and is a fully programmable systems instrument. The frequency range is 1 uHz to 21 MHz and is determined by a precision frequency reference. The phase of the output signal can be precisely controlled +/- 719.9 deg. with 0.1 deg. resolution, and multiple HP 3325As can be locked together for multi-phase application.
